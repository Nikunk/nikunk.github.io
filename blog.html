Foundation for Beginners: Custom Forms and Switches

Written by Naveen Kumar on 1 April 2014. Posted in Blog

Following on Zurb's Foundation , let's now take a look at custom forms and switches. During this tutorial we're going to work on an exercise; a contact form which you can add to your projects or just play around with. Once I built that, we'll move onto other form elements.

The Contact Form

Forms, for me at least, have always been a bit of a pain especially when getting them to fit into My NavdiskZ designs. Foundation makes this whole process easier by including a JavaScript custom form plugin. It's very simple to setup and gives you the ability to easily customise your form elements using CSS.
